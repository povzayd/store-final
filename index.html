Our Heritage

Preserving the Soul of Classical Ayurveda

Established in 1958 in Kachaura by Mr. Satendra Raghav, Satyam Ayurvedics was born from a vision to preserve and promote authentic Ayurvedic formulations through traditional methods.

Today, that legacy is proudly managed by Dr. Satyam (BAMS), who bridges the wisdom of the past with a modern scientific approach. We ensure the highest standards of purity, safety, and effectiveness in every preparation.
